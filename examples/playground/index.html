InSitue Playground

A dependency-free page that mounts the InSitue capture widget with no React, no bundler, no framework. The pill (bottom-right) should appear immediately; clicking it activates the picker, and picks go to the local companion's WS.

Sample region

This card, its heading, and the button are all selectable targets. Click the InSitue pill, then click anything in this card to capture it.

If picks aren't reaching anything: run pnpm --filter @insitue/companion dev in another terminal (from the repo root), then reload.
