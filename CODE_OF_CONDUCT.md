# Code of Conduct

We expect everyone participating in this project — issue reporters, contributors, maintainers — to behave respectfully. This means:

- **Be civil.** Disagreement is fine; personal attacks aren't.
- **Assume good faith.** Most miscommunications are misreadings, not bad intent.
- **Stay on the substance.** Critique the code or the idea, not the person.
- **Be patient with new contributors.** Everyone was new to something once.

The maintainers will moderate issues and pull requests with these expectations in mind. We may close, lock, or delete comments that violate the spirit above. Sustained or severe violations may result in being blocked from the repo.

## Reporting concerns

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this expectation, email **conduct@insitue.com**. Reports stay confidential; we'll acknowledge within 5 business days and take what we judge to be appropriate action.

## Scope

This applies to interactions in this repo (issues, PRs, discussions, commit messages), in project chat or forums we set up later, and to representations of the project in other public spaces.

---

This is a deliberately short document. It's based on the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) but written in our own voice to keep it readable. If you want a formal reference, the full Contributor Covenant v2.1 is the standard most projects use.
